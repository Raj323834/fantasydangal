Responsible Gaming

FantasyDangal respects your passion for gaming. We have thus put powerful measures for you to follow your game and be the best at it. This player-centric initiative of ours is called Responsible Gaming for Fantasy Games and it offers a set of carefully crafted rules that you should abide by to continue playing on our portal.

We intend to make your experience more conducive and more interesting every day through this initiative. Please find the striking features of our Responsible Gaming Policy for Fantasy Games below:

  • Our Fantasy Gaming Services are completely skill-based endeavors.
  • While they may serve as a mode of entertainment and have attractive reward structures, they cannot and must not be equated to a regular source of generating income.
  • You should keep a tab on the amount of time and money that are investing to use our services. Should you believe it is affecting you personally and/or financially, we suggest you stop immediately.
  • Play from a positive state of mind and do not play to recover your “losses.”
  • Please do not borrow money from anyone to play these games.
  • Do take your family and friends in confidence before continuing further.
  • We also restrict players less than 18 years of age to use our services. Please note that if you are an adult, you may not let any minor play through your FantasyDangal User ID.
  • We recommend you place limits on the time and resources that you could freely invest to play our online fantasy games.
  • We recommend you consider bank management very cautiously and use only with an amount that you could afford to lose in a worst-case scenario.
  • We would also inform you in person if we detect any self-damaging gaming patterns and/or any losses that go in excess of INR 20k.

Self-exclusion

  • Players must be 18 years of age to play skill games for real money.
  • There is an option for players to set the daily and monthly playing limit, by sending us a request from their registered email address. Once set, these limits can be changed again only after 72 hours from the previous change.
  • Players can request for their accounts to be temporarily blocked, if they want to self-exclude themselves for some time.
  • Players can follow our ' Responsible Gaming Guidelines' in order to keep a check on their play behaviour.

Please feel free to call us on our Customer Support to know more about how we exercise these regulations to help you get to the best version of yourself. We are just a phone call or an email away from you.
